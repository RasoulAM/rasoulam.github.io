Rasoul Akhavan Mahdavi

My name is Rasoul. I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Florian Kerschbaum. I'm also a member of the Cryptography, Security, and Privacy (CrySP) lab at the University of Waterloo .

I hold a Masters degree in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Twitter  /  Github

Research

My work is primarily on methods to apply secure computation more efficiently. I focus specifically on Homomorphic Encryption and Secure Multi-party Computation. I have also worked on projects on Private-set intersection, and Differential Privacy.
